This comic is a parody of normal flag designs, a subject previously used by1815: Flagand2528: Flag Map Sabotage. For example, animals are commonly used on flags, and stars are also occasionally on flags. However regular flags don't have themselves on it, and don't have tributes or references to topology.[citation needed]It is designed similarly to thexkcd Phoneseries, with a number of improbable features indicated with labels. The caption of "I think our flag design committee really knocked it out of the park" references the common problem ofdesign by committeewhere a design made without a unifying vision but instead many compromises between competing visions results in overcomplexity, banality, and internal contradictions, all of which are present on this flag.
